INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

Restriction enzyme digestion takes advantage of naturally occurring enzymes that
cleave DNA at specific sequences. There are hundreds of different restriction
enzymes, allowing scientists to target a wide variety of recognition sequences.
For a list of many commonly used restriction enzymes, visit NEB.

Restriction enzyme digestion is commonly used in molecular cloning techniques,
such as PCR or restriction cloning. It is also used to quickly check the identity
of a plasmid by diagnostic digest.
